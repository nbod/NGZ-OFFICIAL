Culture Box

The National Gallery of Zimbabwe continues to offer a mobile cultural workshop. The Culture Box is a set of replicas of selected objects from the National Gallery of Zimbabwe's Ethnographic Collection used to interact with learners of Zimbabwean culture. The learners can touch and feel Zimbabwean traditional cultural objects in this workshop. These are classified as musical instruments, kitchenware, weaponry adornment, games and leisure objects. Schools, groups and organisations can have a booking to have a culture box workshop held at the gallery or their premises.
The Culture Box may also be loaned to schools and organisations.
